Privacy Policy for Posh Pricer

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Posh Pricer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

To provide the features of Posh Pricer, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Data: If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.
  • Transaction Data: When you purchase a subscription or IAP, the transaction is handled by Apple. We only receive a confirmation (receipt) from Apple that the purchase was successful.
  • Search and Market Data: We collect and store the search queries you perform (e.g., brand names, shoe models, or categories). This includes the parameters used to find pricing data.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data via Firebase Analytics regarding how you use the app (e.g., features clicked, session duration) to help us improve the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Posh Pricer.
  • Market Analytics: Analyze search queries to identify market trends, popular brands, and pricing fluctuations in the reselling space.
  • Process your In-App Purchases and manage your subscription status.
  • Improve app performance and develop new features for resellers.
  • Communicate with you regarding app updates or support requests.

4. Third-Party Services

Posh Pricer utilizes trusted third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database hosting, and analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Used for app distribution and payment processing. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services.
